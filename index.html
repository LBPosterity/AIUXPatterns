Artificial Intelligence User Experience Design Patterns

An exploration of the UI/UX patterns applicable when crafting the new wave of AI products.

Why

How do we tackle the next big paradigm shift in UX? The answer is abstracted design patterns. These repeated & effective design solutions form best-practice. By pealing back the layers we can define these patterns & leverage them effectively in varied contexts.

About

This reference guide is an attempt to catalogue the unique UX design pattern applicable to delivery effective AI experiences. Real world example are great but these abstracted examples help focus on the specific elements & interaction in each pattern, so designers can review & adapt it their product use case.
