Why

How do we tackle the next big paradigm shift in UX?

The answer is by abstracting the design patterns. These repeated & effective design solutions form what we call best-practice. By pealing back the layers we can define these patterns & leverage them effectively in new contexts.

About

This reference guide is an attempt to catalogue the UX design patterns applicable to delivering effective AI experiences. Real world example are useful, however abstracted examples allow us to focus on the core elements & interaction in each pattern, so designers can review & adapt them to their products context.
